## The problem

Investigators and analysts drown in open-source data. The signal — a suspect resurfacing, a stolen asset reappearing for sale, a pattern connecting separate incidents — is buried across dozens of platforms, and by the time a human finds it, the lead is cold. Manual OSINT doesn't scale, and most tools either dump raw data or hide behind enterprise price tags.

## The solution

Hatchet Trace is an automated open-source-intelligence platform that **collects, correlates, scores, and alerts** — turning scattered public data into ranked, actionable leads, delivered the moment they appear.

### What it does
- **Automated collection** across a wide range of public and social sources.
- **Correlation engine** that links related entities, events, and listings others would miss.
- **Relevance scoring** so analysts see the strongest leads first, not a firehose.
- **Real-time alerting** (including chat/Telegram delivery) the instant a watched subject or asset surfaces.
- **Auto-theft focus** plus general investigative and threat-monitoring use cases.
- **Operator-grade privacy** — optional Tor-routed collection and encrypted credential storage, built by an operator with intelligence and OSINT tradecraft.

## Why it's different

Built by practitioners with real law-enforcement, intelligence, and cybersecurity experience — it reflects genuine investigative tradecraft, not a generic scraper. The value is in the **correlation and scoring**, which improve as the system runs.

## The opportunity

Open-source intelligence is a fast-growing market spanning law enforcement, corporate security, insurance recovery, and threat intelligence. Hatchet Trace is positioned as:
- a **product** for investigators and security teams,
- a **capability acquisition** for an OSINT, threat-intelligence, or public-safety firm.
